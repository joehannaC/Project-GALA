Advocating for a Brighter Future

Give A Love Anywhere (GALA) is a private, non-profit, non-partisan, and non-governmental organization dedicated to promoting an inclusive society through transformative education.

Founded in 2017, GALA operates under the motto: "Founded by love, strengthened by commitment, passion, and determination, driven to share, give, and advocate." This guiding principle underscores their mission to make education accessible and inclusive for all individuals.

Educational Program

GALA offers a range of educational programs aimed at different age groups and communities. These programs are designed to foster inclusivity and provide opportunities for learning and growth.

Community Outreach Program

The organization conducts outreach programs to engage with various communities, identify their educational needs, and provide necessary resources and support.

Advocacy Program

GALA actively advocates for policies and initiatives that support inclusive education. They collaborate with local and international partners to influence positive change in educational systems.

Volunteer Engagement Program

Volunteers play a crucial role in GALA's operations. The organization has a structured process for recruiting, training, and deploying volunteers to various projects and programs.
